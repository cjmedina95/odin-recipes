Elderflower Cocktail

Description

An elderflower cocktail is a refreshing drink made with a syrup or liqueur derived from the flowers of the elderberry tree. This floral and sweet ingredient is often combined with other spirits, juices, and bitters to create a light and aromatic cocktail that is perfect for sipping on a warm summer day.

Ingredients

  • 1oz Vermouth
  • 1oz Gin
  • 1oz Elderflower extract (the one from Ikea)
  • 1oz Lemon Juice (fresh is best)

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients in a 1:1:1:1 ratio.
  2. Enjoy!
